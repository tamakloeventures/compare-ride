Frequently Asked Questions

Are these live fares?
These are estimated fares based on route distance and time. Final pricing and availability are confirmed inside Uber and Lyft.

Do I book inside RideCompare?
No. RideCompare helps you compare quickly, then launches the provider so you can complete booking there.

Why does Lyft sometimes redirect differently on desktop?
Lyft is increasingly app-first. On desktop, RideCompare sends users to Lyft’s app or referral flow instead of a broken web booking page.

Who is RideCompare for?
Anyone who wants to save time comparing rides — commuters, students, airport travelers, and people who simply want a better ride-booking workflow.
